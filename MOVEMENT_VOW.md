# The 5lb Vow: Movement & Recovery

## Daily Movement Vow
- Minimum 30 minutes of intentional activity.
- Focus on "Zone 2" (brisk walking).

## The 2026 Routine
- 10,000 steps daily.
- 10 minutes of stretching before bed.
- 7+ Hours of Sleep: This is where the weight loss actually happens.

## Recovery Vow
- Drink 10oz of water immediately upon waking.
- Cold Shower Finish: 30 seconds of cold water to boost metabolism.
